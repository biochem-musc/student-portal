New Student Check-in Process

Check-in Steps

1
Online Check-in
Upload required documents, download student materials, and check the health check-up schedule at the Online Check-in System.
2
Payment
Pay the new student registration and health check-up fee (Total: 3,000 Baht).

*Required even if documents are still pending upload.

Incomplete Graduation?
If you haven't officially graduated yet, you may submit your degree certificate/graduation certificate and final transcript later via email.
Thai students: gradthai@mahidol.ac.th
International students: gradinter@mahidol.ac.th

Note: You will not be able to activate your Student Service System account for course registration until these official documents are submitted.

Upload Checklist

Formal portrait photo
Degree certificate or graduation certificate
Academic transcript (showing graduation date)
National ID card or Passport copy
Name-surname change certificate / Marriage certificate (if any)
