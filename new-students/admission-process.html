Application Documents

Degree certificate or certification of student status
Academic transcript (English version)
National ID card or Passport copy
Curriculum Vitae (CV)
Statement of Purpose (if any)
Letters of recommendation (if any)
English proficiency score report (if any)

Qualifications

Plan A1: Research only
  • Hold a Bachelor’s degree in science, pharmacy, medicine, dentistry, veterinary, or health sciences (accredited by MHESI).
  • Cumulative GPA ≥ 3.00 on a 4-point scale.
  • Minimum 2 years full-time research experience OR one publication in international peer-reviewed journal OR passed SCBM 372, SCBM 374, SCBC 322, SCBC 421 with average grade of B.
  • Exceptions: Differing qualifications may apply with approval from the Program Committee and Dean.
Plan A2: Coursework and Research
  • Hold a Bachelor’s degree in science, pharmacy, medicine, dentistry, veterinary, or health sciences (accredited by MHESI).
  • Cumulative GPA ≥ 2.50 on a 4-point scale.
  • Exceptions: Differing qualifications may apply with approval from the Program Committee and Dean.
Plan 1.1: Research only (M.Sc. Holder)
  • Hold a Master’s degree in biochemistry, molecular biology, chemistry, or related health sciences.
  • Cumulative GPA ≥ 3.50 on a 4-point scale.
  • At least one publication in an international peer-reviewed journal (Scopus database).
  • Meet English proficiency requirement of the Faculty of Graduate Studies.
  • Exceptions: Differing qualifications may apply with approval from the Program Committee and Dean.
Plan 2.1: Coursework and Research (M.Sc. Holder)
  • Hold a Master’s degree in biochemistry, molecular biology, chemistry, or related health sciences.
  • Cumulative GPA ≥ 3.50 on a 4-point scale.
  • Meet English proficiency requirement of the Faculty of Graduate Studies.
  • Exceptions: Differing qualifications may apply with approval from the Program Committee and Dean.
Plan 2.2: Coursework and Research (B.Sc. Holder)
  • Hold a Bachelor’s degree in science, pharmacy, medicine, dentistry, veterinary, or health sciences.
  • Cumulative GPA ≥ 3.50 on a 4-point scale.
  • Meet English proficiency requirement of the Faculty of Graduate Studies.
  • Exceptions: Differing qualifications may apply with approval from the Program Committee and Dean.
