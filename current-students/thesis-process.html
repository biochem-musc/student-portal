Thesis Process

Thesis Progress Submission

Regular updates on your research milestones submitted at the end of each semester.

Thesis Proposal Examination

Proposing the feasibility and methodology of your research.

Appointing Thesis Title & Advisory Committee

Official registration of your major advisor and co-advisors along with your thesis title.

Research Ethics

Obtaining Certificate of Ethics Approval or Exemption.

Changing Thesis Title/Committee

Process for officially updating your thesis title or modifying your advisory committee.

Thesis Defense Examination

The final oral examination of your completed research work, open to the general audience.

Checking Thesis Format

Ensuring your thesis adheres to the strict formatting guidelines of the Faculty of Graduate Studies.

Thesis Signature Pages

Collecting official digital or physical signatures from all committee members and the Dean.

Turnitin Originality Report

Generating the required similarity report to verify academic integrity before submitting e-thesis.

Submitting the e-Thesis

Final upload of the formatted thesis to the Faculty of Graduate Studies.
