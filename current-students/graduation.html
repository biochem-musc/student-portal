Preparing for Your Graduation

Graduation Requirements

  • Study time should not exceed the study plan
  • Complete 36 thesis credits
  • Meet the English proficiency requirements for graduation
  • Pass the thesis defense (open to general audience) and submit final version of the thesis to FGS
  • One Publication published in an International Peer-Reviewed Journal. Student must be First Author.
  • Study time should not exceed the study plan
  • Complete 18 course credits and 18 thesis credits with GPA ≥ 3.00
  • Meet the English proficiency requirements for graduation
  • Pass the thesis defense (open to general audience) and submit final version of the thesis to FGS
  • One Publication published in a peer-reviewed journal OR presented at an academic conference with peer-reviewed proceeding
  • Study time should not exceed the study plan
  • Complete 48 dissertation credits
  • Pass the Qualifying Examination
  • Meet the English proficiency requirements for graduation
  • Pass the dissertation defense (open to general audience) and submit final version of the dissertation to FGS
  • Two Research Articles published in a peer-reviewed journal. At least one must be in an International Peer-Reviewed Journal. Student must be First Author for both.
  • Study time should not exceed the study plan
  • Credits & GPA (≥ 3.50):
    • Bachelor’s entry: 24 course credits + 48 dissertation credits)
    • Master’s entry: 12 course credits + 36 dissertation credits)
  • Pass the Qualifying Examination
  • Meet the English proficiency requirements for graduation
  • Pass the dissertation defense (open to general audience) and submit final version of the dissertation to FGS
  • One Publication published in an International Peer-Reviewed Journal. Student must be First Author or Corresponding Author.
