e-Form Submission Guide

GR39 GRAS39 GR1 GR49 GR2

Follow these instructions carefully when using the e-Form system.

1. Thesis Proposal / Thesis Title

Provide the English title only. For the Thai title field, enter a hyphen "–".

2. Thesis Abstract

Upload the abstract in English to both the "Thai Abstract" and "English Abstract" fields within the system.

3. Special Characters & Names

If your title contains special characters (e.g., α, β) or scientific names, please list them clearly in the Remark field.

4. Invitation Letters

Select "Need" for all committee members and specify their full academic position.

Example: Associate Professor, Lecturer, Dr.

5. Asking for Permission

Select "Need" for faculty members outside the Biochemistry Department and specify the position of their supervisor. For the Biochemistry Department members, select "No Need".

Note: Specify the position of their supervisor (e.g., Head of the Department of Biology, Faculty of Science, Mahidol University, or Dean of the Faculty of Science, Chulalongkorn University).

6. Curriculum Vitae (CV)

Request a formal CV from faculty members outside Mahidol University. It must include their educational background and publication list.
