Travel Grant Support

The Biochemistry Department provides financial support for attending conferences or reimbursing travel costs via bench fees (if applicable).

MSc Students (ID 63+) Up to 5,000 THB
PhD Students (ID 63+) Up to 10,000 THB
MSc and PhD Students (ID 62 and below) Up to 5,000 THB (Only research supply fee exempt)
SUBMISSION DEADLINE: BEFORE MAKING ANY PAYMENTS AND AT LEAST 30 DAYS PRIOR

Submit these documents to P’Mod for formal approval before making any payments:

  • 1. Project for attending conference/training/seminar
  • 2. Tentative travel schedule
  • 3. Conference details (flyer, agenda, or website)
  • 4. Acceptance letter from organizer (if any)
Important Note:
Thai students must prepare a Thai version of the project file (Item 1).
International students must prepare an English version of the project file (Item 1).
