Appointing Thesis Title & Advisory Committee

Critical Timeline: You must have your thesis title approved and committee appointed AFTER taking your proposal exam and BEFORE submitting any part of your thesis for conferences or journal publications to be used for graduation.
1. Form Submission (GR1)

Once you have passed the proposal exam without the conditions and your proposal exam result is updated in the system, submit the GR1 form via the e-Form system.

e-Form Portal e-Form Submission Guide
2. Committee Composition

Number of committee members:

• For MSc Students: At least 2 members
• For PhD Students: At least 3 members

Committee Qualifications

  • Mahidol Faculty: Have at least 3 academic works in past 5 years (including at least one research work).
  • New Mahidol Faculty: Have at least 1 academic work in past 2 years OR 2 in past 4 years (excluding publications completed as part of their doctoral studies).
  • Outside Mahidol: Have at least 5 publications in the international databases (excluding publications completed as part of their doctoral studies).

  • Outside Mahidol: Have at least 5 publications in the international databases (excluding publications completed as part of their doctoral studies).
