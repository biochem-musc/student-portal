Changing Thesis Details

If you need to update your thesis title, adjust your committee members, or reschedule a pre-approved exam date, please follow the specific procedures below.

Case 1

Changing the Thesis Title

1
Submit GR49 via the e-Form System
e-Form Portal
2
Prepare Documents:
  • New thesis title (English only)
  • Updated thesis abstract (English only)
Note: The research scope must remain consistent. If the research objectives change significantly, you must cancel the thesis title and re-take the thesis proposal examination.
Case 2

Changing Committee Members

1
Download & Fill Form
Download GR49 Form
2
Prepare Supporting Evidence

Create an MS Word file comparing existing vs. updated lists. For new members, include their updated CVs (mainly consisting of education background & publication list).

3
Obtain signature from your Major Advisor.
4
Email the signed form and supporting documents to P'Mod: kritsadapan.tak@mahidol.ac.th
