Thesis Signature Pages

After generating your thesis chapters via the Thesis Format Online System, you must manually verify and prepare the Entitle Page and Approval Page for physical or electronic signing.

Important: Verify Automated Information

The Thesis Format Online System often generates incorrect information or fails to retrieve all committee details (especially thesis defense members). Do not print the automated version without checking against the official templates below.

Verification Checklist

Capitalization: Ensure the Thesis Title is written in ALL CAPITAL LETTERS.
Scientific Names: Ensure any scientific names are in Italic Font.
Margins: Top/Left margins must be 3.75 cm. Bottom/Right must be 2.5 cm.
Committee Names: Cross-check academic titles (Asst. Prof., Assoc. Prof.) and name spellings for all committee members.
Dean Information: Ensure the Dean of Faculty of Graduate Studies and Dean of Faculty of Science are correctly named.
