Research Ethics

Mandatory Requirement: Every research project must have either a Certificate of Ethics Approval (COA) issued by the corresponding Ethics Committee or an Exemption Document.
Step 1
Download Checklist

Download the official self-assessment form to determine your research category.

Download Self-Assessment Form
Step 2
Filling out the Form

Fill out the form to evaluate if your research activity involves human subject research.

Filling Instructions

  • Title of Project: Specify student's thesis/dissertation title (English only).
  • Principal Investigator: Specify the student's name.
  • Signatures: The student must sign as 'Principal Investigator' and the major advisor must sign as 'Thesis Advisor' on Page 3.
  • Format: Electronic signatures are fully acceptable.
Step 3
Submit via e-Form

Submit your completed self-assessment form through the e-Form system.

e-Form Portal

Human or Animal Research

If the assessment indicates your project involves human or animal subjects, you must upload the official Certificate of Ethics Approval (COA) issued by the Ethics Committee to the e-Form system.
