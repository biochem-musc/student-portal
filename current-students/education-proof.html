Proof of Education Request

Follow the timeline below to see which documents are available at each stage of your studies.

After Completing Coursework

Once all required credits for coursework are finished:

Grade Report Certificate of Coursework Completion

After Passing Defense Exam

Following the successful defense of your thesis/dissertation:

Grade Report Certificate of Passing Defense Exam

After Submitting GR5 (Degree Request)

While your application for degree approval is pending with the Mahidol University Council:

Grade Report Certificate of Awaiting the Approval of Graduation by University Council

After Degree is Officially Granted

Once the Mahidol University Council has officially conferred your degree:

Official Transcript Certificate of Graduation

Ready to Request?

All official documents must be requested through the Faculty of Graduate Studies online portal.
